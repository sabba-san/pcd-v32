{% if current_user.user_type == 'homeowner' %}

home Homeowner Guide

Step-by-step workflow to report defects and generate your tribunal report.

  1. 1

    Create a Project & Upload 3D Scan

    Go to the Lidar Scans page and create a new project. Upload your 3D scan file to get started.

    upload_file Go to Lidar Scans
  2. 2

    Launch 3D Visualizer & Pin Defects

    Open the visualizer, click directly on the 3D model to pin defects. Mark their location, type, and severity.

    view_in_ar Launch Visualizer
  3. 3

    Go to Reporting Dashboard

    Select a lawyer from the list and fill in your claim details. All fields must be completed before proceeding.

    bar_chart Reporting Dashboard
  4. 4

    Generate AI Tribunal Report

    Review the auto-generated report, download the PDF, and file it with the tribunal.

{% elif current_user.user_type == 'developer' %}

engineering Developer Guide

Manage your assigned cases and keep homeowners updated.

  1. 1

    Check Dashboard for Pending Cases

    Your dashboard shows all cases assigned to your company. Prioritize by urgency and severity.

    dashboard Open Dashboard
  2. 2

    Update Rectification Status

    Set status to WIP (Work In Progress) or Done. Add a deadline for completion.

  3. 3

    Review Photo Evidence

    Each defect has a photo attached. Click on the photo icon to view evidence before closing a case.

  4. 4

    Submit Completion Report

    Once all items are fixed, submit the report. Homeowners will be notified automatically.

{% elif current_user.user_type == 'lawyer' %}

gavel Legal Guide

Review, verify, and generate legally binding tribunal reports for your clients.

  1. 1

    View Assigned Cases

    Your dashboard lists all cases explicitly assigned to your firm by homeowners.

    folder_open View Cases
  2. 2

    Launch Evidence Review Console

    Open the evidence panel for each defect. Review all photos and notes submitted by the homeowner.

  3. 3

    Verify or Override AI Risk Assessment

    The AI provides a preliminary risk score. Manually verify or override it based on your legal judgment.

  4. 4

    Generate Final PDF Report

    Download the legally binding report. File it with the appropriate tribunal body for your client.
